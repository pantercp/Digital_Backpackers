Welcome to Digital Backpackers

We apire to provide the ultimate resource for digital nomads! We provide travel tips, expense breakdowns, country-specific guides, and remote working tips and tricks. Whether you're a seasoned digital nomad or just starting out, Digital Backpackers is the perfect place to find the information and inspiration you need to live your best nomadic life. So, pack your digital backpack and join us on this exciting journey!

Who are we?

Hi, we’re Christian & Mariam, a couple from the UK who decided to give life on the road together a go back in 2019. Between us, we graduated university, left a job and sold a house to make all this possible. And while Covid did cut our initial adventure in Asia short, we were lucky to travel the Balkans throughout 2021. Currently, we’re working our way through Central & South America!
